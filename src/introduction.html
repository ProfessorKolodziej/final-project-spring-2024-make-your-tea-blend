Before we start...

Tea makers blend various ingredients to create unique teas that we come to love and enjoy. Artisan blenders take things a step further and create tea blends that are proprietary to a tea brand or for individuals.

You can create your own tea blend based on several choices of teas, infusions, herbs and spices. You can get as wild and funky as you want; however, the flavor is the end goal in any blend creation.

I will guide you through creating your one and only tea blend, step by step.
